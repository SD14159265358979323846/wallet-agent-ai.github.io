1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Wallet-Agent-AI handles information when you use the application. By using it, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. No Personal Data Collection

Wallet-Agent-AI is a decentralized application and does not collect, store, or process personal user data. No names, emails, passwords, or identity information are required to use the service. We don't use cookies, tracking scripts, or any analytics tools that collect personal information.

3. Wallet-Based Interactions

All interactions are performed using Radix wallets. Wallet addresses may be visible on-chain as part of standard blockchain operations.

4. On-Chain Data

Transactions executed through Wallet-Agent-AI are recorded on the Radix public ledger. This data is immutable and publicly accessible as part of blockchain infrastructure. We do not control or modify on-chain records.

5. Security

While Wallet-Agent-AI is designed with security in mind, users are responsible for securing their wallets and private keys.
